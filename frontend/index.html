    📋 How this tool works

    This tool generates a VBKREQ XML (Vendor Booking Request) for submission to E2open TA and DavisTurner. The process runs in five steps:

    1 · Supplier uploads Excel
    →
    2 · Carrier ASN fetched from Azure Blob
    →
    3 · Master Workbook built
    →
    4 · VBKREQ XML generated
    →
    5 · Uploaded to E2open SFTP

    Step 1 — Supplier Excel: The supplier fills in the template (download from Step 1). One row per SKU. All red columns are mandatory. Factory address and booking dates must be completed by the supplier.

    Step 2 — Carrier Feed: The DavisTurner carrier ASN XML is either fetched automatically from Azure Blob Storage (keyed on PO numbers parsed from the supplier sheet) or uploaded manually. This feed provides the ASN ID, supplier codes, shipping point, FC destination, country of origin, and line-level SKU quantities.

    Step 3 — Master Workbook: The app merges supplier and carrier data row-by-row (matched on PO + SKU). A MASTER sheet is produced. Unmatched rows from either source are also captured. The workbook is downloadable as an audit trail.

    Step 4 — VBKREQ XML: The MASTER rows are read and structured into the E2open VBKREQ schema. All field sources are documented below. The XML filename follows the pattern DAVIESTN_E2ASOS_VBKREQ_1.0_<timestamp><ctrl>.xml.

    Step 5 — SFTP upload: The generated XML is pushed to the E2open SFTP server. The booking gets reflected in E2open TA against the ASN shipments and DavisTurner will also receive the booking request through E2open.
